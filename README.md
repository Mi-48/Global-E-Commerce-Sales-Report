Global-E-Commerce-Sales-Report

Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing global e-commerce sales performance. The report provides insights into sales trends, profitability, customer segments, regional performance, and operational drivers such as discounts, shipping costs, and payment methods.
The goal of the project is to demonstrate how business intelligence tools can transform raw sales data into actionable insights for decision-making.

Report Overview:

1. Executive Overview\
Provides a high-level summary of business performance.\

Key insights include:
- Total Sales\
- Total Profit\
- Profit Margin\
- Monthly sales and profit trends\
- Sales performance by product category\

3. Performance Analysis\
Analyzes how different regions, customer segments, and products contribute to overall performance.\

Key visuals include:
- Sales, Profit, and Profit Margin by Customer Segment\
- Regional performance by Product Category (Matrix)\
- Top 10 Products by Profit\
- Lowest Profit Products\

This page helps identify high-performing segments and underperforming products.\

3. Operational Insights\
Focuses on operational factors affecting business performance.\

Key visuals include:
- Profit Margin and Sales by Discount Level\
- Sales by Shipping Cost Band\
Sales by Payment Method

Sales and Order Count by Region

These insights help evaluate pricing strategies, logistics costs, and customer payment preferences.
